7) Log in/Register

Target Audience:

The target audience for this component would be anyone who is already a member or would like to become member of the website.

Client/User Needs:

The main goal or purpose of the log in/register page would be the "opening door" of the webpage so to speak. This will allow anyone to sign in/up and give them access to features limited to members only.

Critique:

The layout is simple and easy to use. The color "blue" creates a calming and professional atmosphere for the user. The color "blue" is considered to be one of the most used colors when creating or designing buttons, so I found this color to be best to use since it's favored by most and users will be more willing to access the website.
